The AI back-office that runs your store

Alera is a crew of AI agents that answers your customers, checks every reply against your rules, and escalates anything about money to you — on the channels you already use.

Listen → Understand → Check your rules → Draft → You approve what matters → Done

Answers customers for real

Reads Telegram & email, drafts replies in your tone, and sends the routine ones automatically.

Your rules are the brain

Never discounts past your limit, never ships when you're closed — and tells you which rule when it declines.

Money always waits for you

Refunds and invoices are drafted and pinned for one-tap approval. Nothing about money fires on its own.

Answers from your real catalog

Product and price questions are answered from your actual store data — never made up.

Built as an agent org, not a chatbot

A manager plans each request, specialists execute, a rule keeper gates every send, and every step is traced with its cost.

Manager + specialists

The manager routes each message to the right worker and reviews the draft before it goes out.

Full observability

Every run is a trace tree — who did what, tokens, cost, and latency per step.

Memory that persists

Remembers each customer's history and your policies across every conversation.

Live web research

Pulls current facts (shipping, duties) into replies when the answer isn't in your data.
